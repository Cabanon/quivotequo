Foodstuffs for human consumption: amending certain 'Breakfast' Directives

A9-0385/2023 - Alexander Bernhuber - Article 1, après l'alinéa 1 Directive 2001/110/CE Article 4, alinéa 1 - Am 55

Détail de l'amendement

In Article 4, paragraph 1 is replaced by the following: " For the purposes of the second paragraph of Article 9 of this Directive, the Commission may shall , taking into account international standards and technical progress, establish, by means of implementing acts that adopted are in accordance with Regulation ( EC EU ) No 882 2017 / 2004 625, methods of the analysis European to Parliament verify and that of honey complies with the Council, provisions set of out this methods Directive. of The analysis Commission shall be assisted by a dedicated European Union reference laboratory to verify be whether established honey in is accordance compliant with the Title provisions III of this Regulation Directive (EU) 2017/625 . Those implementing acts shall be adopted in accordance with the examination procedure referred to in Article 7(2) of this Directive not later than four years from the entry into force of this Directive . Until Pending the adoption of such methods, Member States shall, whenever wherever possible, use internationally recognised validated methods of analysis , such as those approved by the Codex Alimentarius to or, verify where compliance appropriate, others in accordance with the Article provisions 34 of Regulation (EU) 2017/625, as amended, to verify compliance with this Directive. "
